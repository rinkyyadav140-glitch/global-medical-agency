Our Products – Category-wise

Generic Medicines

High-quality generic medicines for doctors and chemists. Affordable, effective, and widely trusted. Examples: Paracetamol, Amoxicillin, Ibuprofen, Metformin, etc.

These are bioequivalent to branded drugs but cost less. Available in various forms like tablets and syrups. We ensure compliance with international quality standards.

Ethical / Prescription Medicines

Branded and certified medicines available only with a doctor's prescription. Used for chronic illnesses and specialized treatment. Examples: Heart medicines, Diabetes medicines, Antibiotics, etc.

These require a prescription to ensure safe usage. We partner with leading manufacturers for quality assurance and offer a wide range for cardiovascular, endocrine, and infectious diseases.

OTC (Over-The-Counter) Products

Medicines and healthcare products available without prescription for minor illnesses and daily health care. Examples: Painkillers, Cold & Cough remedies, Vitamins, Antacids, etc.

Convenient for self-treatment of common ailments. Always consult a doctor if symptoms persist. Our OTC range includes multivitamins and digestive aids for everyday wellness.

Baby Care Products

Safe and trusted medicines and products for infants and toddlers. Examples: Baby vitamins, Baby paracetamol syrup, Diaper rash cream, Infant formula, etc.

Formulated with gentle ingredients. Suitable for newborns and up to toddlers. We prioritize hypoallergenic and pediatrician-approved products for your little ones' health.

Ayurvedic / Herbal Products

Natural and herbal remedies for health and wellness. Examples: Ashwagandha capsules, Triphala tablets, Herbal oils, Chyawanprash, etc.

Based on traditional Ayurvedic principles. Promotes holistic health without side effects. Sourced from organic herbs for immunity, digestion, and stress relief.

Surgical / Consumables

High-quality surgical and medical consumables for hospitals, clinics, and chemists. Examples: Bandages, Gloves, Syringes, Thermometers, etc.

Sterile and disposable items for medical use. Compliant with international standards like ISO and FDA. Essential for surgical procedures and daily medical needs.
